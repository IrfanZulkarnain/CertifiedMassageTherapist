    Chiropractic Therapy

    A system of complementary medicine based on the diagnosis and manipulative treatment of misalignments of the joints, especially those of the spinal column, which are believed to cause other disorders by affecting the nerves, muscles, and organs.

    Sport Massage

    Sports massage is proven to release and reduce tension in muscles, improve circulation and encourage the removal of waste products such as lactic acid during high intensity activity and can help to relax the body. All of this helps promote recovery and reduces the chance of muscle injury.

    Reflexology Therapy

    Known as zone therapy, is an alternative medical practice involving the application of pressure to specific points on the feet, ears, hands, head, & neck. This is done using thumb, finger, and hand massage techniques.

    Physiotherapy

    Treatment to restore, maintain, and make the most of a patient's mobility, function, and well-being. Physiotherapy helps through physical rehabilitation, injury prevention, and health and fitness using exercise methods.
